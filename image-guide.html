✈ WanderPlan – Image Guide

Add the following images to the images/ folder. You can download free images from Unsplash.com or Pexels.com.

Required Images (9 destination photos)

  • images/hero-bg.jpg — A wide scenic travel photo (sky, landscape, or city). Suggested: aerial view or beach sunset. Unsplash search: "travel landscape"
  • images/bali.jpg — Bali temples or rice terraces. Search: "Bali temple"
  • images/paris.jpg — Eiffel Tower or Paris streets. Search: "Paris Eiffel Tower"
  • images/manali.jpg — Snow mountains or pine forest. Search: "Manali Himalayas"
  • images/maldives.jpg — Overwater bungalows or turquoise water. Search: "Maldives overwater bungalow"
  • images/kerala.jpg — Backwaters or houseboat. Search: "Kerala backwaters"
  • images/rome.jpg — Colosseum or Vatican. Search: "Rome Colosseum"
  • images/rajasthan.jpg — Desert palace or fort. Search: "Rajasthan palace"
  • images/coorg.jpg — Coffee plantation or waterfall. Search: "Coorg coffee plantation"
  • images/tokyo.jpg — Tokyo skyline or temple. Search: "Tokyo city"

Optional Extra Gallery Images

  • images/beach2.jpg — Goa beach. Search: "Goa beach"
  • images/mountain2.jpg — Munnar hills. Search: "Munnar tea plantation"
  • images/city2.jpg — Dubai skyline. Search: "Dubai skyline"
  • images/placeholder.jpg — Any generic travel image (used as fallback if an image is missing)

Tips

  • Keep file sizes under 500KB each (compress at tinypng.com if needed)
  • Ideal dimensions: 800×500px or wider
  • File names must match exactly (lowercase, no spaces)
  • Images still display nicely even if missing — a placeholder is used
